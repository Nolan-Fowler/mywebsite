Frequently Asked Questions

Are PureSteep tea bags really plastic-free?
Yes! They are made from 100% bamboo pulp with plant-based adhesives.
Do they affect taste?
Not at all. The bamboo pulp is neutral and won’t alter the flavor.
How do I store them?
Keep in a cool, dry place away from direct sunlight for best freshness.
